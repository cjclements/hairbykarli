Welcome

When you enter my suite, I want you to feel like you’re at home. That’s why I focus on meeting your individual needs by getting to know you first with a thorough consultation. This allows me to personalize each hair experience with a color and style that will fit into your busy life—all while ensuring you have what you need to care for your hair at home. I’m committed to providing luxury salon services for eco-conscious women, offering only the highest quality, clean, and sustainable services and brands. I believe in kindness towards all people, our planet, and of course, your hair.
